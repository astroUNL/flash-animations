Variable Star Labs

CCD Mini Simulator

This simulator demonstrates the linear response and potential for saturation of a CCD, and shows how these features relate to the bit depth and exposure time.

Photometry Simulator

This simulator introduces the student to the idea that an image is an array of numbers. It also shows how these numbers can be used to estimate a star's relative brightness.

Registration Simulator

This mini-lab demonstrates that images need to be properly aligned — registered — before further analysis.

Blink Comparator Simulator

This lab is used to determine which stars are variable. At an early stage, but functional.

Variable Star Photometry Analyzer

In this lab students perform PDM analysis on variable stars to determine their periods. With a correct period the lightcurve can be studied to determine the type of variable. Other features of period analysis, such as one-day aliasing, can also be studied with this simulator. At present there are five variable stars in the star field: two Cepheid stars, two RR Lyrae stars, and one eclipsing binary.
